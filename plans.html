Our Plans

Our insurance devision currently has 2 available plans. Both at low, affordable prices.

ITEM ONLY INSURANCE:

This is our cheapest plan, starting at only 10 emeralds.

What is covered?

Your personal items that are on you (Ex. Armor/Sword). HOUSE AND CHESTS ARE *NOT* COVERED WITH THIS PLAN.

ON PURCHASE YOU WILL NEED TO PROVIDE THE FOLLOWING:

- SCREENSHOT OF YOUR PERSONAL ITEMS (ARMOR/SWORD(etc) AND THEIR SPECIFIC ENCHANTS IF APPLICABLE

FULL COVERAGE:

This is our top insurance package, starting at 50 emeralds.

What is covered?

Your house, personal items, and chest items are all covered in this plan.

ON PURCHASE YOU WILL NEED TO PROVIDE THE FOLLOWING:

- A SCREENSHOT OF YOUR HOUSE

- COORDS TO YOUR HOUSE (REQUIRED FOR MAINTENANCE)

- SCREENSHOTS OF YOUR PERSONAL ITEMS (ARMOR/SWORD(etc) AND THEIR SPECIFIC ENCHANTS IF APPLICABLE)

- SCREENSHOTS OF THE ITEMS INSIDE YOUR CHESTS
